20 Benefits of Social Media Marketing

1. Global Reach: Out of 7.7 billion people in the world, 3.5 billion people are on social media. About half the world’s population is accessing the web through their mobile phones. Considering the number of people you will be able to reach through online platforms, seems quite fascinating and practical as compared to banner ads. This makes social media the best possible way to reach billions of people out there. People from innumerable demographics, religions, countries, communities, etc are united by social media. Your business can find a lot of clients/consumers solely through social media. You never know people sitting in the Middle East or maybe in the United States might find your business to their liking. And that’s how your business can go from local to international within a few clicks and searches. Considering this as one of the most important benefits of social media marketing you can dive and thrive in the digital platform with an expert at your disposal.

2. Trackable/ Measurable Results: Advertising through banners and other print media is easy. But do you know how many people actually considered reaching out to your business after seeing that big banner on the national highway? I guess it wouldn’t be a big number, right? The benefits of social marketing are immense. The one reason because of which the demand for social marketing has increased is due to its measurable results. You can track each and every interaction that happens with your business. And since each and every interaction is trackable you can manage your ad campaigns accordingly. With so many options and filters available to manage your promotions that too keeping everything in a budget, you can manage every ad campaign that you plan on conducting. This trackability of social media helps in continuous improvement. Tracking and measuring the ROI of each and every campaign helps you improve upcoming offers and deals.

3. Lower Costs: Companies spend millions of dollars worldwide on banner ads oblivious to the fact that about 90% of people ignore the banners while driving. So you see a lot of money is and gets wasted in advertising which doesn’t even get good engagement. But that is exactly the benefit of promoting on social media, that the cost is almost negligible. Social media marketing allows you to reach out to many users at the same time and that too with a cost-effective method. All you have to do is set up your business profile on various social media platforms which is absolutely free. You can plan a marketing strategy and your own tactics that lie in your budget. Building a community is something that requires social media marketing skills. Brand reputation and community takes time to build and has a high ROI. Through social media, you can gain immense monetary benefits if done strategically. Carrying out ad campaigns under the guidance of an expert can give you a great return of investment through advertising solely.

4. Personalisation: Have you heard about personas in user experience? If not then let us define that for you. User personas are basically outlines of users which are created keeping in mind the different kinds of people who might engage with your business. Everybody likes attention and when your users get that extra attention, they get more engaged with your business. With SEO you can target the users who see your profile. By improving targeting you can get more resources for your marketing budget. For example, by using Google ads you can expand your marketing expense since google ads pay per click. Also if your business running out of budget you can restrict your advertisements to a specific niche of audience. So one of the advantages of promoting on social media is that you can target specific users by giving out specialized offers according to their needs and expectations on various social media sites.

5. Openness: Users/customers/clients expect the businesses to be transparent about there whereabouts. There are times when a minor inconvenience can cause businesses to lose a lot of customers/clients. Social media platforms are all about building an organic community for your business. Once you start engaging with people on social platforms that they are most familiar with, you create a natural bond with them. This creates the loyalty of users towards you. Some benefits of social marketing include building a brand reputation for your business amongst the masses. The more open you are about your achievements and taking feedbacks, the better input you will have in case there is a scope for improvement. Users are the fuel to your business, and it’s necessary to uphold their expectations. Just in case you screw up, social media is an amazing platform for you to confess and apologize for any kind of mishap that might have happened on your end. Create value through social media to add value to people’s lives.

6. Improved Conversion Rate: Nowadays when Instagram and Facebook have allowed you to advertise your business online, so by bringing advertisement to their doorstep you can create awareness about your business. Instead of going out, people like to receive deals on their fingertips or at their doorstep. Once the services or deals that you offer reach the users, they are bound to check out and seal the deal. About 40% of users use social media to find new brands. And about 90% of users do not have any prior brand in their mind while searching on Google. So if you have great SEO, you can land up on their feed in the first go. That’s the benefit of using social media marketing. So if you’re a startup or local/small business social media advertising can benefit you by increasing your conversion rates to manifolds. There are literally uncountable benefits of social media marketing for small businesses. It’s all about using the resources according to your needs.

7. A/B Testing Becomes Easier: Adding sugar to the cake batter is easy but once it is baked nothing can be done. Similarly while preparing an app/website it’s important to study user personas and create something unique and user friendly. When you run online campaigns you get insights about user interactions. These insights help you determine the faults in your campaigns and shortcomings in your business. After analyzing and paying close attention to details and insights you can plan the upcoming marketing targets for your business. These insights are also beneficial for businesses looking for opportunities to expand their reach. For startups, these insights are like goldmines. About 76% of marketers fail to incorporate behavioral data in their online ad targetting which eventually leads to a downfall in the expected results. With prior insights, it’s easier to fix glitches in the beginning than doing it in the end. This will help your business save a huge amount of money, resources, and of course the most expensive utility which is TIME.

8. Powerful Form of Advertising: Advertising effectively helps create awareness about your business. There are numerous modes of advertising available but to find the aptest and efficient one can be a tiresome job. The one platform where you can find the most interactive audience might be tough to find. But hey, that’s why you have marketing agencies to help you with the process. Social media promotion advantages include advertising on the correct platform to receive great conversion rates. Considering the fact that the pandemic is still going on, how do you expect to reach out to people when they are not even stepping out? The one and only way are to advertise digitally and keep your promotions going despite the coronavirus. Digital platforms are convenient and are the best way today to connect to billions of people all over the world. So even if you’re short on budget for advertising, sparing some money to find the correct digital marketing team can help you utilize and monetize each and every social platform according to your needs.

9. Social Media is Ruling the World: According to statistics, by 2020 one in three people have a social media account o which they are active. Out of 7.7 billion people, 3.5 billion people are on social media, and this data is expected to grow to 4.4 billion by 2021. So that’s the power of social media. And once you start advertising and building a community online you can actually build a loyal consumer base for yourself. Social media is being opted by millions of companies and businesses. Whether you are a multinational company or a local/small business if people can find you on social media they are bound to visit your site/store. Just like people check out reviews online before ordering food from a particular restaurant, similarly, if people find you on various social media handles, there is a high probability that they will refer/visit/engage with your business instantly.

10. Reach Out to Potential Buyers: Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is one of the most powerful tactics of social media marketing. By optimizing your content online, you can reach more and more buyers online. SEO is all about using relevant keywords and hashtags. Using relevant keywords automatically helps your business reach people looking for similar things. This also directly promotes more traffic to your site and eventually directing greater leads to your business. The benefits of social media marketing for businesses are huge if done in a planned and strategic way. You need not be present on each and every social media platform available, you just need to find the one where you can connect to your users in the most feasible way. Try hiring a person or maybe a team to look out your social media marketing so that you can focus your energy on other channels.

11. Find Users at the Beginning of their User Journey: User journeys involve quite a few numbers of steps. From searching on Google to closing a deal everything encapsulates the user journey. In a recent study, it was actually shown that 59% of consumers prefer to check several channels of a particular business before making their mind up to visit the business website. This presents a great opportunity for businesses to connect with potential clients and educate them at the very beginning of their user journeys. So if you have good content on your social media handles and it is SEO optimized then you can increase your visibility online and reach out to many users. The whole game is about optimizing, producing, and creating relevant and original content. As they say “your first impression is your last impression”, so you must make sure that your content leaves a forever mark on your visitors so that they keep coming back. Originality is what stands out on social media, so make sure you have a unique mission for your business which is clearly communicated.

12. Reduced Customer Acquisition Costs: CAC is basically the cost incurred in getting clients. In layman’s language, it is the cost of marketing. Businesses spend a huge truckload of money on print advertisements forgetting the fact that it is a complete gamble. It’s very difficult to analyze/collect data as to how the print advertisement helped your business generate leads. In this era, when social media is dominating the whole wide world, advertising on the traditional means doesn’t sound profitable. Whereas social media being completely free can acquire a lot of customers for your business. The expense for advertising is negligible through online platforms and as told before the ROI is just mind-blowing. With an efficient social media marketing team, you can reduce the costs of marketing through other mediums and channel your investments in the right direction. Considering the advantages of social marketing seriously your business can break boundaries in the initial phase itself.

13. Compete with the Best: As social media takes over people’s lives your business can squeeze out the opportunities it has to offer. If your search every single successful brand has a thriving social media presence. The community and user loyalty they receive is major because of the consistent efforts they put into keeping their profiles interactive and engaging. Millions of users like to see their favorite brands take up new initiatives and about 84% of users expect brands to create content. This explains how important brand engagement is. Users expect content that engages them through storytelling, provides useful information/facts and creates entertaining and everlasting experiences. So to create that social media presence, you can always take inspiration from your competitors. A piece of free advice would be not to steal the exact idea but to add a tinge of your own. That’s how you make a mark in between so many brands competing for a spot amongst the top.

14. Stay upto date with the Ongoing Trends:Trends are set through global reach via social media. The Kiki challenge once became famous all over the globe because of social media. Today the top brands on Instagram are seeing a per-follower engagement rate of 4.21% which is 58 times higher than Facebook and 120 times higher than YouTube. With so high engagement rate, Instagram has become the most accessible and trending social media platform for businesses. Social media helps you stay up to date with trends that are going viral. Through social media marketing, you can analyze the kinds of businesses your users interact with. The prior knowledge of this can help you devise your upcoming products, services, ad campaigns, offers, deals, etc that your business can offer. People like staying connected with brands that are up to date with ongoing events and have an opinion to share. By posting simple stories about how you feel about a certain thing can catch the attention of many users. Moreover, as your engagement increases, traffic to your website also increases.

15. Meet Customer Expectations:71% of consumers who are happy with the customer service they get via social media are likely to recommend that brand to others. Social media is one of the most effective and accessible places to solve your consumers’ problems. Not even problems, but through the comments, likes, shares, and saves that your posts receive you can have first-hand feedback. Through feedback, you can improve your user designs. Since improvement is an everlasting process, continuous analysis of consumer interaction through social media can catalyze your improvement. Having a great feedback system o social media can really attract a huge number of customers. Everybody likes businesses that stay in touch with their customers. Meeting consumer expectations is one of the most crucial things that businesses need to maintain. Thankfully one of the pros of social media marketing is the immediate responses that the users are able to receive about their queries. Interactions are easier on social media since it is a familiar platform for users.

16. Gather Real Time Data and Improve: Social media earlier was very basic. It was difficult to track data or gather any insights. Fast forward to 5 years and we have well-defined dashboards for businesses/users to collect, modify, interpret, and analyze all the interactions that occur with their profile. When you gather data, it becomes easy to change certain things. The insights provided by Facebook and Instagram help you determine the KPIs, budget, design, etc for any upcoming ad campaigns. Since data is real-time improvement can take place simultaneously. With increasing data, social media companies are also adding new and exclusive features for their users. Building a content strategy before going live can help you keep a check that everything is in place. All you need are analytical skills and time to utilize each and every feature available.

17. Tell Your Brand Story: Each advertisement that is displayed on huge banners or printed in national newspapers cost a fortune. The print media restricts you to pour your heart out. You can only select concise ad copies/captions that will promote your brand. And often because of this the brand’s mission statement is overshadowed with big ad captions. But social media has the power to communicate the actual brand story. You can put up innumerable posts highlighting your business’ journey, the idea that led to it, and what you seek to deliver in the future. Users love to read about the real story and get to know about the business they are going to associate themselves with. Stories, reels, feed, quizzes, etc everything can help you convey your brand/business mission. Consumers who connect to the authenticity of the business are more likely to stay a loyal customer throughout their user journey. Storytelling is one tactic that can come handy when you plan on sharing your brand story.

18. Drive Traffic to your Business Website: Social media promotions can assist in landing customers directly to your site. Search engines cannot directly bring you customers or traffic to your site. To increase the potential reach of your business you need to spend some time on different platforms. Social media platforms are immense and user engagement is top-notch. Instead of relying completely on search engines, you can leverage various social media sites to get you traffic. Social media channels allow for numerous diverse traffic streams. Although social media channels can be monetized you need to make sure that the content is up to date and is posted at the time when a lot of users are present online. This can be determined by testing for initial time till you get a hand on it. Within your posts, try linking your business site so that people can directly visit you. The more convenient is the step the easier it is for people to search and reach out to your website.

19. Link Building: Link buIlding is all about spreading your content so that people can reach out to you. Consider that you posted an amazing article that got about 1000 shares. Now your followers who might be writing a similar article will put in a link so that people can drive back to your site. Now 1000 shares is a big number to achieve but aren’t impossible if your content is original and solid. Try creating original content on various social channels. This will lead to hundreds of backlinks. Do not focus on a single social media platform rather try to leverage each and every platform possible. This is one of the indirect advantages of using social media to advertise. Links help to build a community and spread the much-needed awareness about your business.

20. Time efficiency: Time is said to be an expensive currency. When people decide to take their business online especially on social media, the one thing they misinterpret is the power and efforts it takes to market on social media. Social media marketing is one of the most efficient forms of advertising and can give you a great ROI only if you do it in a planned way. That’s the only reason the demand for social media marketers is increasing. You might be efficient at multi-tasking but the best way to get good returns of your efforts is to get it done through someone who is aware of the nuance of social media.



Taking a look at the above advantages, social media marketing is going to be future of advertising, so this is the right time to take your business online and reach out thousands of potential people who might be searching exactly what your business has to offer.
